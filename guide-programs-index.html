Catalog

Home / Catalog

The official record of UC Berkeley’s courses, programs, and academic policies is organized into two catalogs: Undergraduate and Graduate. Use the links below to access these catalogs for the current academic year.

 

  • Undergraduate Catalog
  • Graduate Catalog

Questions

Please reach out to catalog@berkeley.edu with edits or corrections.

Archives

Archives of past academic years can be accessed at the top of each catalog using the “Archive” link.

If you are looking to search for available classes each semester (and class times), please see our UC Berkeley Class Schedule, classes.berkeley.edu.
